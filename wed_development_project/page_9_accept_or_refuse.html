Level 3

Opening your eyes, you look around. A long hallway to your right and left, stretching for as far as the eye can see. In front of you you is a faded wooden door, the door repeated down both ends but this door in particular catches your attention.

Tape to it is a small single piece of paper that looked like it was ripped out of an old book. On it is a drawing of a poppy and some text underneath

[Papaver rhoeas] "In the language of flowers, a poppy means-"

The last word is too smudged to make out. Turning the page around, there's more text.

"Now that you know, isn't it like an exit?"

Know what? you scoff. You know in the back of your mind something isn't right but what if it's a way out? You MUST choose.
